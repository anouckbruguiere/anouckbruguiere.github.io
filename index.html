About Me

My name is Anouck Bruguière, and I am a multitasking young lady. In my spare time, I play the harp, but I’m also a failed figure skater (shameful side quest).

I’m originally from the South of France, which provides the perfect sunny lighting for my true calling: obsessing over things that spin. It’s a lifestyle, really, but let me explain.

After dreaming 1,000 different career paths, from professional swimmer to pastry chef, illustrator, and journalist, I decided to naturally, after a social and economic high school degree, choose the most chaotic neutral path possible. I moved to Grenoble for a dual bachelor's in Physics and Music. Why choose one type of suffering when you can have two?

I then pursued a Master’s in Acoustics and vibration in Le Mans, where I discovered my true, unhinged passion: ROTATING MACHINERY. I am now pursuing a PhD thesis between Le Mans and Grenoble, focusing on the vibes (literally) of:

  • Water turbines (splashy)
  • Fans (windy)
  • Ball mills (literally just rocks in a blender)

I’m essentially one element away from becoming the Avatar.
"Is it a man’s world?" maybe. But why leave all the fun to the men? Honestly, if you’ve never stayed up until 3 AM analyzing the raw vibration signal of a Francis turbine, are you even living?
