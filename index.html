Anouck Bruguière

My name is Anouck Bruguière, and I am a multitasking young lady. In my spare time, I play the harp, but I’m also a failed figure skater (shameful side quest).

I’m originally from the South of France, which provides the perfect sunny lighting for my true calling: obsessing over things that spin. It’s a lifestyle, really.

After dreaming 1,000 different career paths, from pastry chef to journalist, I naturally chose the most chaotic neutral path possible: a dual bachelor's in Physics and Music. Why choose one type of suffering when you can have two?

During my Master’s in Acoustics, I discovered my unhinged passion: ROTATING MACHINERY. I am now pursuing a PhD focusing on the vibes (literally) of:

  • Water turbines (splashy)
  • Fans (windy)
  • Ball mills (rocks in a blender)

I’m essentially one element away from becoming the Avatar. "Is it a man’s world?" maybe. But why leave all the fun to the men? Honestly, if you’ve never stayed up until 3 AM analyzing the raw vibration signal of a Francis turbine, are you even living?
